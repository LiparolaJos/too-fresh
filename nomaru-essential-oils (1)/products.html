Our Products

Three carefully crafted essential oils, distilled from South Africa's finest botanical sources.

Rose Geranium Oil

Our Rose Geranium oil is steam-distilled from the leaves and stalks of the Pelargonium graveolens plant, native to South Africa. It offers a sweet, floral aroma with a hint of mint.

Benefits

  • Skin balancing and rejuvenating
  • Aromatherapy for relaxation and stress relief
  • Natural, elegant fragrance

Typical Uses

Add a few drops to your facial moisturizer, use in a diffuser for a calming atmosphere, or blend with a carrier oil for a soothing massage.

Castor Oil

Cold-pressed from the seeds of the Ricinus communis plant, our Castor oil is a thick, nutrient-rich oil packed with fatty acids and Vitamin E.

Benefits

  • Promotes hair, lash, and brow growth
  • Deep skin hydration and moisture locking
  • Excellent natural carrier oil for blends

Typical Uses

Apply directly to scalp or skin, use as a base for essential oil dilutions, or incorporate into DIY hair masks.

Lemon Grass Oil

Extracted from the fresh or partially dried leaves of Cymbopogon citratus, our Lemongrass oil has a powerful, fresh, and grassy-citrus scent.

Benefits

  • Refreshing and uplifting scent
  • Natural cleaning and deodorizing properties
  • Aromatherapy for energy and focus

Typical Uses

Diffuse to purify the air, add to natural cleaning solutions, or use in a foot bath for a refreshing experience.
