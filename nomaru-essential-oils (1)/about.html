About NOMARU

NOMARU is a small artisanal essential oil manufacturer based in South Africa. We believe in the power of nature — sourcing botanicals from local farms and using traditional extraction methods to create oils of exceptional purity. Every product reflects our commitment to sustainability, quality, and the rich botanical heritage of South Africa.

Natural Extraction

We use steam distillation and cold-pressing to preserve the therapeutic properties of every plant.

Sustainable

Our farming and production processes are designed to minimize environmental impact.

Local Sourcing

We source our botanicals from local South African farmers who share our values.

Our Process

1. Plant Sourcing

We hand-select the finest botanicals at their peak potency from organic farms across South Africa.

2. Distillation

Our small-batch distillation process allows us to monitor quality at every second, ensuring purity.

3. Quality Control

Every batch is tested for its chemical profile and therapeutic standard before being bottled.

Our Mission

"To provide the world with the purest South African essential oils, fostering wellness and sustainability through every drop we produce."
